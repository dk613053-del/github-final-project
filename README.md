# Paradise Nursery - Online Plant Shop

An interactive, user-friendly e-commerce shopping cart application designed for plant lovers to browse, select, and manage their favorite greenery online.

## Project Details

*   **Application Name:** Paradise Nursery
*   **Core Objective:** To apply dynamic web development skills to build a functional online shopping experience featuring intuitive cart management and responsive interactions.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Plant Catalog:** A dynamic showcase of plants featuring high-quality images, distinct names, detailed descriptions, and individual pricing.
    *   **Interactive Navigation Bar:** Easy switching between the plant nursery view and your cart, complete with a live cart item counter.
    *   **Dynamic Cart Management:** Add plants to the cart, completely remove items, or seamlessly increment/decrement item quantities.
    *   **Real-Time Cost Calculations:** Total costs automatically update as users modify their cart contents.

## Tech Stack
*   HTML5 & CSS3
*   JavaScript (Dynamic DOM Manipulation & State Management)
